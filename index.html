    IMPORTANT MEMORIES

    Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was born on 15 October 1931 to a Tamil Muslim family in the pilgrimage centre of Rameswaram on Pamban Island, then in the Madras Presidency and now in the State of Tamil Nadu. His father Jainulabdeen was a boat owner and imam of a local mosque.His mother Ashiamma was a housewife.

    • Born

      He was born on 15 October 1931 and raised in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu.

    • Achivement

      Bharat Ratna in 1997.Hoover Medal in 2009.NSS Von Braun Award in 2013

    • President

      He was elected as the 11th President of India in 2002 with the support of both the ruling Bharatiya Janata Party and the then-opposition Indian National Congress.

    • Died

      27 July 2015 (aged 83) Shillong, Meghalaya, India
